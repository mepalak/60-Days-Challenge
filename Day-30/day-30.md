# 🚚 Day 30 – Build a Supply Chain Optimizer

## 📌 Overview

Welcome to **Day 30** of my **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**!

Today's project focused on building a **Supply Chain Optimizer**, an AI-powered web application that helps organizations improve the efficiency of their supply chain operations. The application analyzes logistics data, inventory levels, transportation costs, supplier performance, and demand forecasts to recommend optimized strategies for reducing costs and improving delivery performance.

This project demonstrates how AI can assist businesses in making smarter operational decisions while maintaining customer satisfaction and supply chain resilience.

---

## 🎯 Objective

Develop an interactive Supply Chain Optimizer that enables users to:

- Analyze supply chain performance
- Optimize inventory allocation
- Reduce transportation costs
- Improve supplier selection
- Forecast customer demand
- Identify operational bottlenecks
- Generate AI-powered optimization recommendations

---

- 📦 Inventory optimization dashboard
