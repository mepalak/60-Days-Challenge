## 🚀 Project Overview

Today, I built a **Meeting Notes Intelligence Dashboard** using Claude AI.

The dashboard transforms messy meeting transcripts, brainstorming notes, and multiple team members' meeting notes into structured, actionable insights. It helps teams quickly identify action items, decisions, owners, and conflicting information from meetings.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Convert raw meeting transcripts into structured summaries.
- Generate speaker-wise meeting insights.
- Extract action items automatically.
- Detect conflicting information between multiple meeting notes.
- Assign ownership and priorities.
- Present everything inside a clean interactive dashboard.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎙️ Transcript Summary Mode
- 📝 Brain Dump Notes Analyzer
- 🔄 Merge Multiple Meeting Notes
- ⚠️ Conflict Detection
- 👤 Speaker-wise Decision Tracking
- ✅ Automatic Action Item Extraction
- 📊 Priority & Status Filtering
- 🌐 Fully Client-side HTML Dashboard
- 📱 Responsive Design

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Claude AI
- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript

---

## 📚 Key Learnings

- AI can convert unstructured meeting notes into organized knowledge.
- Summarization becomes more valuable when combined with action extraction.
- Detecting conflicts between multiple versions of meeting notes improves team communication.
- Assigning clear ownership increases accountability.
- Interactive dashboards improve readability and productivity.

---

## 💡 Key Insight

Two people attending the same meeting may interpret discussions differently. AI can identify these inconsistencies before they become project risks, ensuring better collaboration and decision-making.
