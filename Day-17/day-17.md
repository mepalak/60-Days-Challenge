# 🚀 Day 17 – AI Interview Simulator with Claude

> **60 Days of Claude Challenge**

## 📌 Overview

On Day 17, I explored how Claude can act as an intelligent interview coach by simulating realistic technical and HR interviews. Instead of simply generating questions, Claude adapts based on responses, provides constructive feedback, and highlights areas for improvement.

This exercise demonstrates how AI can make interview preparation more interactive, personalized, and effective.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Practice technical and HR interview questions
- Receive instant feedback on responses
- Improve communication and problem-solving skills
- Build confidence before real interviews

---

## 🧠 What I Learned

- Structuring effective interview prompts
- Using role-based prompting for realistic conversations
- Asking Claude to evaluate answers like an interviewer
- Identifying strengths and improvement areas
- Practicing follow-up questions for deeper understanding

---

## 💡 Prompt Used

```text
Act as a Senior Software Engineer conducting an interview for a Frontend Developer role.

Your responsibilities:
- Ask one interview question at a time.
- Wait for my answer before continuing.
- Include technical, behavioral, and situational questions.
- Evaluate my answer on:
  • Technical Accuracy
  • Communication
  • Confidence
  • Problem Solving
- Give constructive feedback.
- Suggest a stronger answer if needed.
- End with an overall interview score and improvement tips.
```
