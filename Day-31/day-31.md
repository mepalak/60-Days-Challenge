Day 31 – Build an AI Supply Chain Control Tower
📌 Overview

On Day 31 of the 60 Days of Claude AI Challenge, I built an AI Supply Chain Control Tower—an intelligent dashboard that provides end-to-end visibility into supply chain operations. The application monitors inventory, logistics, supplier performance, warehouse status, and shipment risks while using AI to generate actionable insights and recommendations.

This project demonstrates how AI can help businesses proactively identify disruptions, optimize operations, and improve decision-making across the supply chain.

🎯 Objective
Build an AI-powered Supply Chain Control Tower.
Visualize real-time supply chain KPIs.
Monitor inventory, shipments, suppliers, and warehouses.
Detect risks and operational bottlenecks.
Generate AI-driven recommendations for supply chain optimization.
