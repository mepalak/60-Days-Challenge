Day 27 – Prior Authorization Workflow Simulator
📌 Overview

On Day 27 of the 60 Days of Claude Challenge, I built an interactive Prior Authorization Workflow Simulator using Claude AI. The application simulates how healthcare insurance prior authorization requests move through different workflow stages, helping users understand the complete approval process from patient intake to the final decision.

This project demonstrates how AI can rapidly generate complex workflow-based applications with drag-and-drop interactions and realistic healthcare scenarios.
