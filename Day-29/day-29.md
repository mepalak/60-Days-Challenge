# 🚀 Day 29– Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

## 📌 Challenge Overview

On **Day 29** of the **#60DayClaudeChallenge**, I built **Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab** using Claude AI.

This interactive simulation places users in the role of a supply chain decision-maker responsible for managing critical logistics during emergencies. The application challenges users to respond to disruptions, allocate resources efficiently, and maintain the flow of essential supplies under pressure.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To simulate real-world supply chain crisis management by enabling users to:

- Monitor supply chain health
- Identify disruptions and bottlenecks
- Prioritize critical shipments
- Allocate limited resources
- Respond to emergency scenarios
- Minimize delays and operational risks
- Maintain continuity of essential services
