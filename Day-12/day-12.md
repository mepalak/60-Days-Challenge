## 🎯 Objective

Learn how to use **Prompt Chaining** to solve complex tasks by breaking them into smaller, connected prompts instead of asking everything in a single request.

---

## 🧠 What is Prompt Chaining?

Prompt Chaining is a technique where the output of one prompt becomes the input for the next prompt.

Instead of giving one large prompt, you divide the workflow into multiple logical steps. This improves accuracy, clarity, and reasoning.
