# Day 14 – Build AI Workflows with Claude

## 📌 Objective

Today focused on understanding how to build structured AI workflows using Claude. Instead of relying on a single prompt, I explored how to break complex tasks into multiple logical steps, allowing the AI to produce more accurate, organized, and reliable outputs.

---

## 🎯 What I Learned

* What AI workflows are and why they matter.
* Breaking large problems into smaller, manageable tasks.
* Designing step-by-step prompt sequences.
* Improving response quality through structured interactions.
* Reducing errors by validating outputs between workflow stages.

---

## 🛠️ Activities Completed

* Studied the concept of AI workflows.
* Created a multi-step workflow using Claude.
* Tested the workflow on different tasks.
* Refined prompts to improve consistency.
* Documented observations and key takeaways.

---

## 💡 Key Takeaways

* Complex problems are easier to solve when divided into smaller steps.
* Structured workflows produce more consistent AI responses.
* Clear instructions at each stage improve accuracy.
* Iterative refinement leads to better results than one-shot prompting.
